Research

My research focused on both applied and quantitative ecology—the application of statistical and computational methods to understand ecological patterns and processes. I focus on movement ecology and road ecology, and I’m especially interested in study design, and the development of reproducible workflows to support ecological decision-making. I am also interested in understanding the direct and indirect impacts of roads on vertebrate communities.
